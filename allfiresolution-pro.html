Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Still have questions? We answer within 2 hours on WhatsApp.

💬 Ask on WhatsApp
Which types of fire extinguishers do you supply?+
We supply all ISI-certified types — DCP (Dry Chemical Powder), CO₂ (Carbon Dioxide), Foam, Water, and Clean Agent extinguishers. Available in 1kg to 50kg capacity. We also supply fire buckets, hose reels, and sprinkler systems for industrial use.
How often should a fire extinguisher be refilled?+
As per Indian fire safety norms, extinguishers should be inspected every 6 months and refilled every year or after use — whichever comes first. Our AMC covers all this automatically with reminders.
Do you provide GST invoices for all services?+
Yes, every job — supply, refilling, AMC, or audit — gets a proper GST invoice. Our GSTIN will be updated on all invoices once registration is complete. Businesses can claim ITC on fire safety purchases.
Which areas in U.P. do you serve?+
We are based in Prayagraj and serve all surrounding districts including Varanasi, Lucknow, Kanpur, Mirzapur, Chitrakoot, Pratapgarh, and Fatehpur. For industrial bulk orders, we serve all of Uttar Pradesh.
What is included in an AMC (Annual Maintenance Contract)?+
Our AMC includes: 2 scheduled inspections per year, full refilling when due, pressure testing, valve & pin replacement if needed, service certificates after each visit, and priority emergency support. Fixed price, no surprise charges.
Can you help with Fire NOC for a new building?+
Yes. We assist with the full Fire NOC process — site inspection, documentation preparation, submitting the application to the U.P. Fire Services Department, and following up till approval. We have experience with hotels, factories, schools, hospitals and commercial buildings.
